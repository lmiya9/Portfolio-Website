About me


Back to Homepage

Hello, I'm Mia, and I'm an aspiring front-end developer from Vancouver, BC. Upon completing my bachelor's degree in Psychology, I've decided to explore different career pathways and became increasingly interested in web-development. I took a leap of faith and completed an Introduction to Coding Workshop, which unveiled the captivating mysteries of this dynamic industry.

Fast-forward to today, I've earned several web development certificates and crafted a portfolio of projects that showcase my proficiency in CSS, HTML, and JavaScript. These skills have not only empowered me but also ignited my passion for the field of front-end development.

Since January 2023, you'll find me at Simon Fraser University, where I contribute as a Course Production Technician. In this role, I thrive on collaborating with instructors to elevate their Canvas courses. My responsibilities encompass crafting customized layouts, incorporating multimedia elements, and ensuring a premium standard of course design.

I'm open to explore new opportunities and potential collaborations.
